Course Description

This comprehensive course covers two essential certifications: CompTIA A+ and CompTIA Network+. In the first part of the course, you will delve into CompTIA A+, where you'll master the core concepts of computer hardware, software, and operating systems. From assembling and configuring PCs to troubleshooting and maintenance, you'll gain the knowledge needed to become a competent IT support technician. You'll also explore topics like networking, security, and cloud computing, preparing you for real-world challenges in the IT industry.

In the second part, we'll cover CompTIA Network+, where you'll learn the essentials of network technologies, protocols, and topologies. Understanding network infrastructure, managing network devices, and securing networks will become second nature as you progress through our engaging curriculum. By the end of the course, you'll be well-prepared to pass the CompTIA A+ and Network+ exams and equipped with the skills to excel in a diverse range of IT roles.

What you will learn

✓

Computer Hardware and Software: Students will learn about computer components, assembly, troubleshooting, and operating systems.

✓

Networking Fundamentals: The course covers network types, protocols, configurations, and essential networking concepts.

✓

Security and Troubleshooting: Students will gain knowledge of security best practices and how to troubleshoot hardware and network issues.

✓

Mobile Devices and Cloud Computing: The course explores mobile device connectivity and introduces cloud computing and virtualization technologies.

✓

Dual Certification Prep: Participants will be prepared for CompTIA A+ and Network+ certifications, validating their IT skills and enhancing their career prospects.

By the end of this course, students will be well-prepared for IT careers with dual certifications in A+ and Network+. They'll have hands-on experience, problem-solving skills, and a professional network, making them valuable assets in IT support, network administration, and security roles. A bright future awaits in the dynamic world of technology.

Course Curriculum

Week 1&2

Introduction to CompTIA A+

•

Understanding computer hardware components and peripherals

•

Installing, configuring, and troubleshooting PCs and laptops

•

Exploring operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux)

•

Networking fundamentals and concepts

•

Implementing basic security practices

•

Troubleshooting common hardware and software issues

Week 3&4

Advanced CompTIA A+ Topics

•

Configuring and troubleshooting mobile devices

•

Setting up and managing networks

•

Introduction to virtualization and cloud computing

•

Understanding hardware and network technologies

•

Implementing security best practices

•

Preparing for the CompTIA A+ 220-1001 and 220-1002 exams

Week 5&6

Introduction to CompTIA Network+

•

Networking concepts and the OSI model

•

Network infrastructure and cabling

•

IP addressing and subnetting

•

Routing and switching basics

•

Wireless networking and security

•

Network troubleshooting and tools

Week 7&8

Advanced CompTIA Network+ Topics

•

Implementing network protocols and services

•

Wide Area Networks (WAN) and Virtual LANs (VLAN)

•

Network security and policies

•

Cloud and virtualization technologies in networking

•

Preparing for the CompTIA Network+ N10-007 exam

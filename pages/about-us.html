Our Team

Fatuma A. Dubow

Fatuma A. Dubow has a wide experience in community development, having worked with the marginalized North-Eastern communities in Kenya. She is a development specialist with focus on community mobilization, community development, and NGO (Non-governmental organization) partnerships. Her experience as a Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability and Learning (MEAL) expert puts the organization at a strategic position of efficiently using and controlling the available resources. In addition, Dubow has a Master of Arts in Development Studies from the Catholic University of East Africa. She is in charge of Projects Implementation, Monitoring and Learning component of NieHub.

Alinoor R. Hassan

Alinoor Hassan is an Economic development professional experienced in SME, trade and private sector including business start-ups. Previously managed large economic development projects funded by USAID, DFID and World Bank. He has over 10 years of experience in the field of research, particularly conducting qualitative and quantitative research. He has an excellence facilitating and training skills. He is experienced in working with civil organization, government agencies and international humanitarian organization. He holds a Master of Arts in Project Planning and Management from University of Nairobi. Alinoor Hassan serves as the Strategic Planning and Finance Manager for NieHub.

Alinoor M. Bosh

Alinoor Moulid Bosh is an experienced independent journalist, having worked in the Horn of Africa, particularly in Somalia, Kenya and Ethiopia. His specialties are in journalism, editing and online (digital) journalism, making his experience invaluable in implementing the programs of NieHub. Alinoor is an indispensable asset to the organization as he has traversed the north eastern region of Kenya, with strong grassroots connections in the major towns in the region. Alinoor is a graduate of Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in Communication, Journalism, and Related Programs from the Kenya Methodist University in 2015 and a continuing student of a Master of Arts in Diplomacy and International Relations at Kenyatta University. Alinoor serves as the Operations Manager as well as the Communication person at the organization.
